Step 1. Download the 'CapScape.ipa' file in this folder to your laptop/desktop (not iPhone/iPad).

Step 2. Connect your device to your computer via USB. Open iTunes.

*NOTE*: In order for the next step (3) to work you must have ‘Manually manage music and videos’ enabled in your iTunes options. This can be found by clicking the small phone icon in iTunes under the play and rewind/forward button controls. Then scroll down to the ‘Options’ section and you’ll see the checkbox there.

Step 3. Drag and drop the downloaded ‘CapScape.ipa' file to your device name next to the device icon in the ‘Devices’ category in the left pane of iTunes (ex. David's iPhone). You should then see a sync message at the top of the program while it is syncing the app to your device.

Step 4. Look for the app on your device. Since an icon hasn’t been made yet, the default icon is a white background with lines and concentric circles.
